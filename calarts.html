About

Selected projects from the MTIID program at CalArts.

"The Music Technology: Interaction, Intelligence & Design (MTIID) program enables students to build strong musical skills while mastering a variety of technology for the creation of sonic and visual art."

"This unique interdisciplinary program prepares undergraduates and graduates for futures focusing on electronic composition and performance, sound design and synthesis, design, electronics, software development, and interactive audio."
