A/V Art/Code

History of Union Station

2016

About

The Hettema Group(link) is an interactive installation and experiential design firm. In the spring of 2016, the collaborated with a group of CalArts students on brainstorming for an interactive art installation at Union Station in Los Angeles. With a team of five students we conceptualized and prototyped an interactive walkway which harnesses the power of ambisonic sound. Using pressure sensors and an arduino we created panelling which controls an ambisonic sound rig which tracks user movement. As a viewer steps on the panels, sounds are played strategically around the location of the user

In order to celebrate the history of union station, as the user walks through the hallway of speakers, old train sounds are played around the viewer, connecting them to the past of the past of Union Station.
