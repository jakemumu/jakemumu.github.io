Overview

Eventually I've gotten sick of not understading the standard symbols and their mathematical meanings, so here is a list of the symbols I frequently come across and what their meanings are.

I'm not sure how exactly to manage this, so when I come across new equations with symbols I haven't yet understood I'll add them here.
