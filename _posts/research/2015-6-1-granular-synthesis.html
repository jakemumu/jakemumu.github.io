About this project

Granular Synthesis & Unsupervised Synthesis Programming.

This research paper defines an architecture for high quality real time granular sysnthesis with a high emphasis on musicality and synchronous streams of granular audio data.

The architecture defined has a wide range applications and was the bedrock of Output's Portal plugin.

It also explores machine learning in a musical context, and the potential for machine learning to aid in the automatic programming of synthesizer parameters to achieve a target sound.
