About this project

Since 2012, Jacob Penn has been producing electronic music under the pseudonym Mumukshu. He's released on various record labels and compilations through American and Australian labels, and has played concerts all across North America and Europe include festivals such as Sonic Bloom(Colorado), Burningman(Nevade), Freqs of Nature(Germany), Ometeotle(Mexico), and many more.

To learn more about Mumukshu, visit this website.
