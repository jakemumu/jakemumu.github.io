About this project

The twitter audio encryption algorithm was a piece of experimental code developed for the 2015 CalArts Digital Arts Expo. It was around the time of Sony being hacked for producing an anti North Korean comedy film.

Cyber security is an important part of our society. For this project i designed a sender and receiver algoritm. The sender pull tweets pertaining to a keyword off of twitter and converts these messages into OSC. OSC is sent to the chuck programming language, which converts the OSC message into a fast series of sine wave blips.

The receiver algorithm listened to sound waves in the air and attempts to convert frequencies into text, if there is little audio disruption between the sender and receiver, a participant can post a tweet with a keyword, and watch their tweet be converted into audio, played across the room, and reconverted back into text through FFT.
