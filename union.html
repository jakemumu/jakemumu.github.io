Union Station

History of Union Station

2016

About

The Hettema Group is an interactive installation and experiential design firm.

In the spring of 2016, I collaborated with them and a group of five CalArts students on brainstorming an interactive art installation for Union Station in Los Angeles. We conceptualized and prototyped an interactive walkway which harnesses ambisonic sound.

Using pressure sensors and an arduino we created panels to control an ambisonic sound system which tracks user movement. As a viewer walks through the space, sounds are played strategically around them.

Old train sounds are played around the viewer, connecting them to the history of Union Station.
