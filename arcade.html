About this project

Arcade by Output.

"We've been lucky enough to give Output's new Arcade a spin. I'm not going to mince my words on this one... it's simply awesome. Imagine a platform that can live in your DAW as a plugin where you can transform loops in truly unique and imaginative ways, almost always creating something cool-sounding.

But Output didn't stop there. Arcade isn't just a powerful and accesible loop synthesizer and transformer, it's also a platform for new audio content which can be kind of mindblowing when you start to use it."
- Ask.Audio
