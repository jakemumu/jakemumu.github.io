About

As part of my undergraduate work I have independently created various cross platform audio effect plugins. These include modulated delay effects, panning effects, filtering effects, and custom granular engines.

MumuGranular is a custom cross platform granular plugin designed in c++ using the JUCE framework. It is strongly focused on the manipulation of real time signal, and gives the user easy access to complex repitching and time stretching algorithms. The software also has a strong emphasis on percussive granulation of sound, able to sync with host bpms and deliver rhythmic chopping of sounds real time.
