About

Jacob Penn

Musician, Programmer, Curious.

born in 1993 in Minneapolis, Minnesota
works and lives in Los Angeles, California

Jacob Penn is an American electronic musician and creative technologist. Focused on the intersection of art, technology, & engaging experience, his work takes on many forms including electronic music composition, custom A/V software, and interactive art experiences.

Jacob Penn holds a BFA in music technology with a minor in digital arts from California Institute of the Arts.

Skills

Sound Design & Composition

Strong background in music composition and sound design. Educated in western classical and post tonal music theories

Audio Plugin Engineering

Consistently striving for a deeper understanding of modern audio technologies.

Human Computer Interaction

Loves building novel interfaces using the arduino platform.

Creative Code & Web

Experienced with JUCE, openFrameworks, and Processing. Has understanding of web applications with Rails.
