Jacob Penn

Musician, Prorgammer, Artist, Creative Technologist, Curious.

born in 1993 in Minneapolis, Minnesota
works and lives in Los Angeles, California

Jacob Penn is an American electronic musician and creative technologist. Focused on the intersection of art, tech, & transcendental experience, his work takes on many forms ranging from electronic music composition, human-computer interaction, custom A/V software, and interactive art experiences.

Jacob Penn holds a BFA in music technology with a minor in digital arts from California Institute of the Arts.
